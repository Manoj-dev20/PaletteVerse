Beautify a Wall in Your Local Area
Create street art that brightens up your community. Take before and after photos.
Full Description
Transform a blank wall in your neighborhood into vibrant street art. This project aims to brighten communities and make public spaces more engaging. Document your process and the final result.
Rules & Guidelines
  • Must have permission from property owner
  • Project must be completed within 2 weeks
  • Submit photos of before, during, and after
  • Artwork must be family-friendly and community-focused
